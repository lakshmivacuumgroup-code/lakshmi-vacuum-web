Delivering Precision Vacuum Sintering for Advanced Metal Filters


Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies delivered a precision-engineered bottom-loading vacuum sintering furnace for a reputed Indian manufacturer of porous metal filters used in aerospace, pharma, chemical, and gas applications.

Background

The client is a reputed manufacturer of advanced sintered metal filters used in critical applications such as:

  • Aerospace and defense
  • Pharmaceuticals and biotech
  • Chemical processing
  • High-purity gas filtration

These filters are made from stainless steel (SS316L, SS310) and nickel-based alloy powders. The powders are compacted into precise geometries and sintered at high temperatures to form porous yet mechanically robust structures for high-purity applications.

Technical Challenge

  • Maintaining ±3°C temperature accuracy across stacked parts
  • Preventing oxidation during high-temp sintering (1100–1350°C)
  • Preserving pore integrity without collapse or over-sintering
  • Accommodating different filter geometries and batch sizes
  • Requiring vertical loading for gravity-assisted shape retention

Lakshmi Vacuum's Solution

Lakshmi designed and supplied a BLVSF Series Bottom-Loading Vacuum Sintering Furnace tailored to deliver precision, reproducibility, and structural integrity.

Key Furnace Features

  • Furnace Type: Bottom-loading, vertical cylindrical
  • Max Temp: 1250°C with ±5°C uniformity
  • Hot Zone: Graphite + heat shields
  • Vacuum Level: 10⁻³ mbar
  • Backfilling: Argon/Nitrogen inert atmosphere
  • Loading: Hydraulic, programmable lift
  • Controls: PLC + SCADA with recipe management
  • Safety: Overpressure valves, purge cycles, interlocks

Technical Insight: Vacuum Sintering

  • Debinding and degassing of powder compacts in vacuum
  • Solid-state diffusion bonding below melting point
  • Retention of pore size and permeability with improved strength
  • Oxidation prevention using vacuum or inert gas
  • Bottom-loading ensures geometry stability and even heating

Customer Validation

Real production trials were conducted with extensive evaluation:

  • Uniform pore structure across all sintered parts
  • >98% densification without pore blockage
  • Consistent geometry and strength
  • No discoloration or oxidation
  • Process window control within ±2°C

Long-Term Impact

Precision Sintering: High-quality filters, minimal rejects
Bottom-loading Design: Better ergonomics and stability
Gas Flexibility: Sintering of multiple alloys
Custom PLC-SCADA: Full traceability and repeatable cycles
Reliability: >95% uptime for 2+ years

Why Lakshmi Vacuum?

  • Deep experience in PM part and filter sintering
  • Specialized in bottom-loading vacuum systems
  • Turnkey support: design → training → maintenance
  • In-house metallurgy and process development

Conclusion

The client scaled up their high-purity metal filter production with complete control, precision, and confidence—thanks to Lakshmi Vacuum’s advanced sintering platform and engineering support. The BLVSF furnace is now a critical part of their world-class production line.
