Tool Steel

At Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies Pvt. Ltd., our Tool Steel Division is committed to delivering world-class metallurgical solutions to meet the demanding needs of the tooling industry.

We specialize in providing a comprehensive range of Tool Steels from our partners, to cater engineered and processed for superior performance, precision, and durability.

Our Portfolio Includes:


Hot Work Tool Steels

Ideal for applications involving high thermal loads and repeated heating cycles, our hot work tool steels offer exceptional resistance to heat, wear, and thermal fatigue. These grades are widely used in:

  • 01.Die casting
  • 02.Extrusion tooling
  • 03.Forging dies
  • 04.Hot shear blades

Cold Work Tool Steels

Designed for high strength and wear resistance at room temperatures, our cold work tool steels are optimized for precision cutting, stamping, and forming. Applications include:

  • 01.Blanking and punching tools
  • 02.Thread rolling dies
  • 03.Cold extrusion tools
  • 04.Industrial blades

Plastic Mould Steels

Engineered to provide excellent machinability, surface finish, and dimensional stability—our plastic mould steel range meets the critical demands of high-precision injection and blow moulding.

High-Speed Steels (HSS)

For cutting tools requiring high hardness retention and toughness at elevated temperatures, we offer premium-grade HSS tailored for:

  • 01.Drills, milling cutters
  • 02.Taps and dies
  • 03.Broaches and gear cutters

Why Choose Lakshmi Vacuum?

  • 01.Heat Treatment Expertise: With in-house advanced vacuum heat treatment facilities, we ensure optimal hardness, toughness, and microstructure tailored to each application.
  • 02.Custom Solutions: We work closely with customers to offer grade selection assistance, custom dimensions, and pre-machining support.
  • 03.Quality & Traceability: Every batch is processed with stringent quality checks and full metallurgical traceability to ensure long-term performance.
