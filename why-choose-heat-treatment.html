Why Choose Us

Lakshmi Vacuum Heat Treaters Pvt. Ltd. stands out in the field of thermal processing by offering both advanced heat treatment services and in-house manufacturing of state-of-the-art vacuum furnaces. This dual capability provides a unique advantage, ensuring clients receive comprehensive solutions backed by deep technical expertise.

Comprehensive Experience in Heat Treatment

With over 2 decades of experience, Lakshmi Vacuum Heat Treaters Pvt. Ltd. has established itself as a leader in Vacuum Heat Treatment Services across India.

Operating in ten major industrial hubs, the company offers a range of services including vacuum hardening, annealing, stress relieving, brazing, and carburizing. These processes are essential for industries such as aerospace, automotive, defense, and electronics, where precision and material integrity are paramount.

Best-in-class Features of Heat Treatment Services

01
Oxidation-Free Processing:

The vacuum environment prevents oxidation, ensuring clean surface finishes and preserving material properties.

02
Uniform Temperature Distribution:

Advanced control systems maintain consistent temperatures, reducing the risk of distortion and ensuring uniform material properties.

03
Precise Temperature Control:

The ability to accurately control heating and cooling rates allows for tailored heat treatment processes, achieving desired mechanical properties.

04
Environmentally Friendly Operations:

Vacuum heat treatment processes are energy-efficient and produce minimal emissions, aligning with sustainable manufacturing practices.

Manufacturing Par Excellence

In-House Manufacturing of Advanced Vacuum Furnaces

Beyond providing heat treatment services, Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies designs and manufactures its own range of vacuum furnaces, including the VHF (Vacuum Hardening Furnace), VBF (Vacuum Brazing Furnace), and Preci-Carb (Vacuum Carburizing Furnace) series.

Key features of these furnaces include
Compliance with International Standards

All furnaces are engineered to meet standards such as AMS 2750 Latest Rev, ensuring quality and reliability.

Energy Efficiency

Designed with low consumption of electricity, gas, and other utilities, these furnaces are both cost-effective and eco-friendly.

Advanced Control Systems

Equipped with PLC and SCADA systems, the furnaces offer fully automated processes, enhancing precision and repeatability.

Versatility

The furnaces are capable of handling various processes such as quench hardening, annealing, normalizing, and brazing, accommodating a wide range of materials and applications.

Customizable Configurations

Options for horizontal or vertical loading, multi-zone temperature control, and different quenching methods (gas or oil) provide flexibility to meet specific client needs.

Integrated Solutions for Enhanced Value

The combination of heat treatment services and furnace manufacturing allows Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies to offer integrated solutions that provide significant benefits:

Tailored Solutions

Clients receive customized heat treatment processes and equipment designed to meet their specific requirements.

Technical Support and Training

The company provides hands-on training and metallurgical services, ensuring clients can effectively operate and maintain their equipment.

Continuous Innovation

By manufacturing their own equipment, Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies stays at the forefront of technological advancements, incorporating the latest features and improvements into their services and products.

Cost Efficiency

Integrated services reduce the need for multiple vendors, streamlining operations and potentially lowering overall costs.
