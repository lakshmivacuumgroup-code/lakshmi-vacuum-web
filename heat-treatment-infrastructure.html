Infrastructure

At Lakshmi Vacuum Company, we take pride in our state-of-the-art infrastructure that powers our ability to deliver world-class vacuum systems and components. Our integrated facility houses Fabrication, Assembly, and Testing units under one roof, ensuring seamless coordination and superior quality control throughout the production lifecycle.

Fabrication Facility

Our advanced fabrication unit is equipped with modern machinery and skilled technicians capable of handling complex builds with precision. We adhere strictly to international welding standards, ensuring structural integrity and durability in every product.

Assembly & Testing Facility

We have a dedicated in-house assembly and testing section to validate the performance and reliability of every component and system before dispatch. This ensures that only fully compliant and performance-tested systems reach our clients.

Quality Practices

Quality is the cornerstone of our manufacturing philosophy. At Lakshmi Vacuum, we implement stage-wise inspection throughout every phase of production to maintain the highest industry standards.

Our quality assurance measures include:

Radiographic Testing

Ultrasonic Testing

Hydrostatic Pressure
Testing (Hydro Test)

Raw Material Testing
& Certification

These rigorous procedures ensure every component meets structural and operational expectations before it leaves our facility.
