Vacuum Furnaces

Precision Heat Treatment for Advanced Applications

What is a Vacuum Furnace?

A vacuum furnace is a type of furnace that heats materials, typically metals, in a vacuum-sealed environment. It's widely used for high-precision thermal processes such as annealing, brazing, sintering, and heat treating of advanced alloys.

By eliminating oxygen, a vacuum furnace ensures clean, oxidation-free surfaces and superior metallurgical properties.
