Expertise

Lakshmi Vacuum is a pioneer in the field of vacuum technology, offering unparalleled expertise in both vacuum heat treatment services and the design and manufacture of high-performance vacuum furnaces.

With a legacy of engineering excellence and deep metallurgical insight, we serve as a trusted partner to a wide spectrum of industries including aerospace, automotive, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, cutting tools, die & mould, defense, energy, power transmission, and general engineering.

Our heat treatment services are engineered to deliver consistent metallurgical properties, improved component life, and precision-controlled results. With state-of-the-art facilities across India, we specialize in advanced processes such as vacuum hardening, tempering, annealing, aging, stress relieving, solutionizing, and brazing, tailored to meet the exacting standards of each industry we serve.

In parallel, our vacuum furnace manufacturing division is at the forefront of innovation, offering custom-built and standard furnaces that integrate modern control systems, energy-efficient designs, and robust construction. These furnaces are used by OEMs, R&D labs, and captive heat treatment facilities to achieve exacting thermal processes with the highest level of repeatability and reliability.

We take pride in our ability to deliver turnkey solutions—from concept and design to commissioning and after-sales support. With a strong emphasis on quality, customer-centric engineering, and continuous innovation, Lakshmi Vacuum has become synonymous with trust, technology, and thermal excellence across India and beyond.
