Company

Lakshmi Vacuum Technologies has been leading the way with unparalleled expertise in design, manufacturing and servicing in Vacuum Technology. The solutions are comprehensive and integrated, with a focus on being a one-stop hub across industries and requirements.

We have state-of-the-art infrastructure and robust technological capabilities to provide bespoke solutions to diverse industry needs.

Leaders in Vacuum Technology

Lakshmi Vacuum has firmly established itself as a leader in vacuum technology within India since its inception in 2003. Driven by a commitment to indigenizing this critical technology, the company, under the guidance of Mr. L. N. Prasad, rapidly evolved from a heat treatment service provider to a comprehensive thermal solution company.

Lakshmi Vacuum distinguishes itself by offering end-to-end solutions, from raw materials to the manufacturing of state-of-the-art vacuum furnaces and the provision of extensive heat treatment services.

Their innovative approach and dedication to quality have positioned them as a preferred brand across diverse sectors, including aerospace, automotive, FMCG, and tooling, successfully competing with international players and contributing significantly to the advancement of vacuum technology in the region.
