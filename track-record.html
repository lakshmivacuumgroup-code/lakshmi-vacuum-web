Impact

08+ Heat Treatment Units Pan-India Presence

We operate a robust network of 8+ fully equipped heat treatment facilities and multiple manufacturing units across India, strategically located to serve regional and national industrial hubs.

10,000+ Tons of Components Treated Annually

Our heat treatment facilities process a vast volume of critical components each year, maintaining tight metallurgical tolerances, uniform hardness, and distortion control across all jobs.

1000+ Satisfied Customers

We are the trusted partner for over a thousand clients across aerospace, automotive, pharmaceutical, medical devices, cutting tools, energy, defense, and general engineering industries. Our client base includes several Tier-1 and Tier-2 suppliers to global MNCs.

350+ Vacuum Furnace Delivered

As a recognized OEM of high-performance vacuum furnaces, we have successfully designed, manufactured, and installed more than 200 units across India and abroad—ranging from standard horizontal vacuum furnaces to complex custom-engineered solutions for R&D and aerospace applications.
